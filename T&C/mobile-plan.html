Service Description

The Business Mobile Postpaid Plan is a mobile post-paid service with optional value add-on services. The Customer has the flexibility to customize their post-paid service package by opting for cost effective add-on services which can give them extra SMS, international talk time, data, incoming roaming and/or business group calling, within the limits set by Etisalat.

In addition to the benefits stated in Clause 3(a) above, the Service may be bundled with smart phone devices with instalment plans extending over twelve (12), eighteen (18) or twenty-four (24) months. The smart phone devices with these instalment plans may also be provided as add-ons to the Service.
